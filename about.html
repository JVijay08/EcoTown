About EcoTown

Read the paragraphs below to learn about the purpose, mission, and way to use our website.

Our Mission

Our mission for this website is to create an experience for our community that allows them to access any resources that they would need to live their life comfortably. Without these useful resources and opportunities, life can be hard in our community.

How EcoTown Works

EcoTown is a community hub where members of our community can access multiple resources and volunteering opportunities that fit their needs. They can learn about the impact they might be having on the environment, all of this provided on our community website.

Why It Matters

The reason that the EcoTown website exists is to help the residents of our community live happy, green, and stress free lives with our plethora of services that help us serve our community well.
